BAC Advanced Team Art Recommendation System

About our Project

Our topic is creating a model to help identify different classes and styles of art. Unlike songs that can be played and Shazamed, art work is visual and cannot be put into words to help define it. Creating a model that can help people understand the genre of art they enjoy and help find similar pieces would be both interesting and useful to society, helping end frustration in finding art styles.
